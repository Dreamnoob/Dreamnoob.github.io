NFT tokens are a product of pure creativity. Artists work on their art, putting unique images into them. Art in the digital format is now at the stage of building up its potential. Not all of its capabilities are sufficiently appreciated today. Non-interchangeable NFT tokens, like any other objects presented in a single copy, have an increasing value over time.

The volume of transactions in recent years amounts to billions of dollars. Many artists strive to present their works to real connoisseurs, and they face problems along the way:

  • Adequate commission for the issue of NFT

  • Selection of a suitable site for the placement of the collection

    product offer (USP).
