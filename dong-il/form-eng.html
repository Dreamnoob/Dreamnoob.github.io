Personal information policy

Collection of personal information

We collect the following personal information for online inquiry service.

ο Collected items: name, company, phone number, mobile number, email

ο Method of collection: website (product inquiry and quotation)

Collection and use of personal information

We will use the collected personal information for the following purpose.

ο For online consulting

Possession and period of using personal information

In principle, after the collection and purpose of personal information is accomplished, the collected information is destroyed immediately.

However, if the relevant law requires preservation of such collected information, we will preserve the personal information of the relevant user for a period stipulated by the relevant law

Preserved items: name, company, phone number, mobile number, email

Basis of preservation: Law on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce

Period: 5 years

Destruction procedure and method for personal information

In principle, after the collection and purpose of personal information is accomplished, the collected information is destroyed immediately..

ο The procedure and method are as follows

The information you enter for online inquiry is moved to a separate DB (a separate document folder, if it is on a paper) and then preserved for a period according to the purpose of information protection based on the company policy and relevant law (see Preservation and Period of using Personal Information), before destruction. Personal information that has been moved to a separate DB is not used for any other purpose than the stated purpose of preservation, unless it is required by a law.

ο Destruction method

- Personal information saved in an electronic format is deleted by using a technology that does not allow retrieval of record.

- Personal information printed on a paper is shredded by using a paper shredder or incinerated.

Consignment of collected personal information

We will not consign personal information of users without the consent of the users. If it becomes necessary in the future, users will be notified of the consignee and consigned work and, if necessary, asked for consent in advance.
